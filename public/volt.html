Giving Back Time

There is nothing like giving back to the society that made you. Taking some quality time to visit children at the basic schools in my community is one of my hobbies

Most students struggle in the STEM subjects especially with mathematics. I therefore decided to routinely go to neigbouring primary schools and engage with them.

Mathematics made easy

Most of the time, I break down complex mathematical topics for their comprehension.

Building fun projects

On other days we build projects together with available materials and sometimes I give them leadership talks on how to be a responsible person in the community.
