Radio transmitter

Description

In most of my projects I need wireless control so I decided to build this multifunctional 12 channel radio transmitter which can be used for pretty much everything.

It features:

  • NRF24L01 +PA +LNA
  • Arduino Pro Mini
  • IMU MPU6050 (Gyroscope and accelerometer)
  • 2x Potentiometers
  • 2x Joysticks
  • 6x Push Buttons
  • 2x Toggle Switches
  • 2x Potentiometers

Circuit Diagram

I carefully made the schematics with the EasyEDA software. EasyEDA is an online platform that allows you to build schematics and design PCB boards. There are other good options like the artium and AutoDesk Eagle softwares but I choose EasyEDA because it makes designing of schematics, PCB and the ordering of your designed PCB easier.
