An automated device for testing PCB

Description

This is the prototype of my Bachelor's thesis at K.S.P.E.U, Russia. This device was co-designed and built with my college Daniel Henneh (https://www.henneh.com/), currently employed at Apple as Product Design Engineer. The topic was: " DEVELOPMENT OF THE MEASURING PART OF THE DEVICE FOR AUTOMATIC CONTROL OF ELECTRICAL MODES OF PRINTED CIRCUIT BOARDS ". The control deviced used was Arduino Nano. Two stepper motors move in both the vertical and horizontal axis with the help of guide rails. Mechanical limiting switches were used to determine when the motors reached the end of the device to prevent any crash.
